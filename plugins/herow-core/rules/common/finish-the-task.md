## Finish the task

Complete the full task and reasoning before ending your turn. Do not stop early and wait for
a "continue" — if there are remaining steps, keep going until the task is done or you are
genuinely blocked.

- Don't end a turn mid-reasoning, mid-edit, or right after announcing an action you haven't
  taken yet ("I'll now…", "Next, I…"). Take the action, then report.
- For multi-step work, finish every step you committed to, then summarize what changed.
- A real blocker is a legitimate stop: say so explicitly and state exactly what you need.
  Silence, an unbalanced code fence, or a trailing lead-in with nothing after it is not a stop —
  it's an unfinished turn. (A `Stop` hook may bounce those back to you with a reminder to continue.)
